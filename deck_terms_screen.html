Terms

Anatomy

The study of the structure of the human body and the relationship of its parts to each other.

Physiology

The study of the normal function of living organisms and their parts, including physical and chemical processes.

Pathology

The study of the causes and effects of disease or injury, focusing on changes in structure and function.

Pharmacology

The branch of medicine concerned with the uses, effects, and modes of action of drugs.

Histology

The microscopic study of the structure of tissues and cells.
